How to participate

The Contribution Period will start on 6 June 2016 at 3pm GMT and will end on 27 June 2016 at 3pm GMT, or 24 hours after the soft cap has been reached.

To learn details about how the contribution period contract works please refer to this blog post, and to read more on the Status Network Token model structure please refer to Status Network Whitepaper.

Please be advised that the exact start date is determined by a block number. We will announce the exact block number and contribution period contract address once it’s deployed to the Ethereum network - a day before the start of the Contribution Period.

Sending ETH to the Smart Contract address directly from an exchange will likely result in the loss of your ETH. Do NOT do this. You can instead find tutorials below on how to use Parity, MyEtherWallet or Mist to participate.

What is Status?

We’re paving a path towards mass-adoption of Ethereum. Status is the first, and currently the only, mobile Ethereum client built entirely on peer-to-peer technologies.

Status combines a messenger, web 3.0 browser, and a wallet to build a gateway for everyday smartphone users to begin using dApps, and a simple way for dApp developers to reach new users.

If you’d like to try our alpha, Android users try it right now. iOS users need to request an invite via Apple TestFlight from our homepage.

The Status Network Token

The Status Network Token ('SNT') is created during the contribution period, and serves as a crucial part of the Status Application. Our objective is to create a Users-as-Stakeholders network; where the economic incentives of the platform is to serve the users on the platform.

Given Status is a generalized platform, and possesses a broad range of use-cases and native-DApps that in and of themselves could be powered by a token, SNT takes a modular approach, and serves a number of core functions across the network.

SNT forms the the foundation of our content discovery and ranking systems for Status dApps, powers our network-level Acquisition Engine, is used for our Governance & Proposal system. These are outlined in more depth in our Whitepaper.
