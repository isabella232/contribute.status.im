The Status Network Token (SNT) is an ERC20 token on Ethereum’s Mainnet. We issued them during the Contribution Period. You can read about the various use cases for SNT in the Status Whitepaper. You can learn about the capabilities of DApps by watching the Status Intro Video.

If you participated in the Contribution Period, congratulations on joining the Status Network. Thank you for being a part of our early community. We look forward to building the future of Ethereum along with you.

How soon can I transfer my SNT?

You can transfer your SNT to another Ethereum address seven days after the Contribution Period finishes as described in the Terms.

How do I view my SNT?

Here are the instructions on how to view your SNT in different clients:

  • Etherscan
  • MyEtherWallet (MEW)
  • Parity

Etherscan instructions

Open your web browser and navigate to https://etherscan.io. Paste your Ethereum address into the search box in the upper right corner of the page. Click Go. Look for the Token Tracker expandable section. Click [View Tokens Balance & Value] to expand the section. You will see all of your tokens there, including StatusNetwork.

MyEtherWallet (MEW) instructions

Open your web browser and navigate to http://myetherwallet.com. Select the View Wallet Info tab. Import your wallet with SNT using any one of the methods available.

In the right sidebar, locate the third section, Token Balances. You will see all of your tokens there, including SNT.

Parity instructions

Open your browser to the Parity interface. By default, the address is: 127.0.0.1:8180. Click on the Accounts tab. After you’ve imported your address, your account will show the SNT icon next to the ETH balance. Hover your mouse cursor over the [AccountName 0x...YourAddress] account box. The balance of SNT will be shown next to the token icon.
